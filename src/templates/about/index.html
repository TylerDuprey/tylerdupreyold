About Me

I am a web designer and developer. I built this website to teach others as well as strengthen my own skills in web development. I’ve been learning and doing web design for about 8 years now.

I am accepting opportunities for new projects to work on. If you’re in need of a highly skilled developer and designer I am available for contract work on a project to project basis. Please contact me here and include a description of yourself, your project and your timeline. I have a general resume posted here but contact me for more details.

My Background

In addition to creating several independent projects, I’ve worked as a web designer for one of the largest banks in the United States. Although, I’ve come a long way as a web developer, I look forward to improving my skills every day.

I’m a graduate from Ohio University’s E.W. Scripps School of Journalism where I studied Public Relations. Despite spending most of my time coding, I have extensive experience in the public relations and digital marketing fields. Throughout most of my time as an employee I’ve worked as a marketer. This experience has and continues to inform my work as a web developer every day.

Life Stuff

I was born and raised in Columbus, Ohio and still spend a lot of time there. However, I now live with my Canadian wife in the Toronto area.

I enjoy the outdoors and spend a lot of time snowboarding, hiking, camping, hunting, fishing and target shooting. When I’m not outside I obviously enjoy web development, and watching my Columbus Blue Jackets (NHL).

Feel free to say hello! I try to respond to every message but I can’t promise I’ll get to everyone. I hope you enjoy the free web development tutorials and happy coding!
